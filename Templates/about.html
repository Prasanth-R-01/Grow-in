About Grow'in

Grow'in is an AI powered System that recommends crop and predicts disease particularly for Kitchen Garden Users. At Grow'in you can get to know some of the top crops that can be grown in your Kitchen Garden based on the Weather and Soil Parameters. Our ML Algorithm is trained in such a way that the results suggested are accurate and reliable. For Disease Prediction, our DL model finds out the affected disease and suggests methodologies to cure that disease. Along with this you can also get to know some of the effective ways in which you could prepare the field and also grow the seed into sapling and these methods are verified by the Resource Person who is in the field of Agriculture.
